The Student Web Awards is a highly anticipated bi-annual competition and showcases of the best in student Web design.

The competition is open to both undergraduate and graduate student work.


Submissions for the awards will be accepted from:

Spring: January 1st - July 1st

Fall: July 2nd - December 31st

Winners will be announced 2 weeks after submissions close for the spring and fall semesters.


This contest is an online exhibition, juried by leading design practitioners and educators. The jurors selected for this event share a passion for design education and a commitment to supporting emerging design talent.

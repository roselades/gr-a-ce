[ W H A T - C A N - Y O U - D O ? ]

1. contact your local government

urge your local representatives to become climate change leaders. voice your opinions and ask them to support green legislation against fossil fuels and fracking, and to pressure large corportations with an enormous carbon footprint to adopt green practices.


2. conscious eating habits

so much of the earth's pollution comes from industrial agriculture. support small to midsize organic farms and buy local, or even start your own garden! participating in "meatless mondays" can significantly impact the environment positively.


3. green your commute

utilize public transportation and carpools when possible rather than driving your own vehicle. if you must use a personal vehicle, switch to a hybrid or electric if possible.


4. minimize energy use

small reductions in energy use here and there can add up and make a large impact. unplugging electronics when they're not in use, energy efficent lights, air drying the laundry, and winterizing your home can all positively impact the environment - and your wallet!


5. reduce & reuse more than you recycle

only so much of what you recycle actually gets recycled. while recycling is great, it'd best on the environment to replace disposable recyclables with reusables. metal straws, reusable coffee cups, reusable food wraps, and even more can help reduce daily plastic waste and impact the environment better than simply recycling does.
